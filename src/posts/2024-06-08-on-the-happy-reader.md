"The trouble with ebooks is that no one else can see what you're reading. Like it or not, books are an astonishingly efficient accessory."

"To love a book is to be overcome by an innate desire to advertise it, so that others might read it and love it too. When there's a book we've enjoyed but can't keep, and we leave it on the communal wall, and then we come back later and it's gone, it's fulfilling in a shimmeringly complex way. Someone else might be reading it: someone else's mind might be becoming just that little bit more connected to our own."
